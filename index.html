ambulance services

Medical and healthcare facilities are a part of essential services in any society. An ambulance service is a significant part of medical emergencies. Situations such as accidents, sudden medical condition, road mishaps, etc. require patients to be ferried to the nearest hospital at the earliest with certain first aid facilities for immediate relief.

emergency rooms

An emergency department, also known as an accident & emergency department, emergency room, emergency ward or casualty department, is a medical treatment facility specializing in emergency medicine, the acute care of patients who present without prior appointment; either by their own means or by that of an ambulance.

free check-ups

Regardless of how healthy a person seems, everyone needs regular health check-ups. It has been advised time and again by doctors that full-body screen once a year is essential to keep track of your health. These health checkups can help detect any possible ailments you are at risk for and take a preventive approach to keep you healthy.
